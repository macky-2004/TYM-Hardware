Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal information and protect your privacy.

Data Protection

Your Privacy Matters to Us

TYM Electricals & Hardware respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with us through our website, phone, WhatsApp, or in person at our shop.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you make an enquiry, place an order, or contact us. This may include your name, phone number, email address, delivery address, and details about the products you are interested in. We also collect information automatically through our website such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited for analytical purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for business purposes including processing your orders, responding to your enquiries, providing product information and price quotes, arranging deliveries, and improving our service. We may occasionally send you updates about new products or offers, but only if you have opted to receive such communications.

Data Protection & Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except when required by law or when necessary to fulfill your order (such as sharing your delivery address with our delivery partner). Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. We retain your information only as long as necessary to serve you and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, and withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at our shop or reach out via phone or email. We are always transparent about our practices and happy to address any concerns you may have.
