Our Journey

The Story Behind TYM Electricals & Hardware

TYM Electricals & Hardware was founded with a clear vision: to create a shop where customers could find genuine electrical and hardware products without the fear of counterfeit goods or misleading advice. What started as a modest outlet has grown into a trusted destination for quality supplies.

Our founder recognised early that the market was filled with cheap duplicates and unreliable products. Customers needed a place they could trust. That trust became the foundation of our business. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with leading manufacturers, allowing us to offer premium products at competitive prices.

Today, we serve a diverse range of customers from individual homeowners to large contractors. Every interaction is guided by the same principles that started it all: honesty, quality, and service.
