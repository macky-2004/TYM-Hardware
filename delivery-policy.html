Shipping & Delivery Policy

Clear terms on how we deliver your orders and what you can expect.

Delivery Terms

Fast, Reliable Delivery When You Need It

At TYM Electricals & Hardware, we understand that time is critical for contractors and homeowners alike. We strive to ensure your products reach you as quickly as possible. Below are our delivery policies and terms.

Delivery Areas

We currently offer delivery within Thanjavur and surrounding areas. For customers within a 10 km radius of our shop at Mathur, we provide free delivery on orders above a minimum amount. For areas beyond this range, delivery charges may apply based on distance and order value. Please contact us to confirm delivery to your location.

Delivery Timelines

We aim to deliver all orders within 24 hours of order confirmation. For urgent requirements, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM, subject to product availability and location. Bulk orders to construction sites and contractor projects are scheduled based on mutual convenience.

Order Process

Orders can be placed via phone, WhatsApp, or in-person at our shop. Once you place an order, we will confirm product availability and provide a delivery estimate. Payment can be made via cash, UPI, or bank transfer. For large orders, partial advance payment may be required.

Order Modifications & Cancellations

You may modify or cancel your order before it is dispatched. Once the order has been dispatched for delivery, cancellation may not be possible. In case of product damage during transit, we will replace the item at no extra cost. Please inspect all products at the time of delivery.
