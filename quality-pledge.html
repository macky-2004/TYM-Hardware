Our Commitment

We Stand Behind Every Product We Sell

At TYM Electricals & Hardware, quality is not just a word on our signboard. It is the foundation of everything we do. We understand that when you buy electrical or hardware products, you are trusting us with your safety, your project, and your money. That trust is something we take seriously.

100% Genuine Products Guarantee

All products we stock are sourced directly from authorised manufacturers and distributors. We do not deal in duplicate, counterfeit, or substandard goods. Every item you purchase from TYM comes with proper billing and manufacturer warranty wherever applicable.

Trusted Brands Only

We partner with India's most trusted electrical and hardware brands including Havells, Polycab, Finolex, Anchor, Legrand, and Orient. Every product bearing these names is verified for authenticity before it reaches our shelves.

Your Safety Is Our Priority

Electrical products directly impact your safety. We never compromise on quality standards. Every switch, wire, and safety device we sell meets the required ISI and BIS standards. You can rely on TYM for products that are safe, durable, and built to last.

If It Is Not Genuine, We Do Not Stock It.
